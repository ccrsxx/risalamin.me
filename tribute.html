Ir. Soekarno

The man behind the declaration of Indonesian independence

  • 1901 - Born in Surabaya, Indonesia.
  • 1916 - During his school years he just went to local elementary school and also his high school in 1016. His friends in the schools are all Dutch.
  • 1920 - Soekarno married Siti Oetari.
  • 1923 - Soekarno divorced Siti Oetari and married with Inggit.
  • 1943 - Soekarno never had children with Inggit, so Soekarno divorced her.
  • 1945 - He married again with Fatmawati. They had five childrens Megawati Soekarnoputri, Guruh Soekarnoputra, Rachmawati Soekarnoputri and Sukmawati Soekarnoputri.
  • 1945 - Soekarno with Bung Hatta declared independence of Indonesia.
  • 1949 - After the transfer of sovereignty on December 27, 1949, the unity which Soekarno kept in the revolution fell apart and now which lead to 3 groups began attacking each other.
  • 1965 - Soekarno's days as president were limited because the communist symphathizers were leading a coup which soon affect Soekarno harshly.
  • 1966 - Removed from power, and Soeharto became President.
  • 1970 - dies at the age of 69 from kidney failure.
